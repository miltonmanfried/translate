\header{August 22nd 1932 (Kaiserhof)}

The Gau organization has been brought to life. Everything must be prepared for the difficult struggles that now stand before us. In a week the preparations will be ready. In Beuthen, several SA men were sentenced to death because they \?{shot down}{niedergeknallt} a Polish insurgent. This sentence is so monstrous that it is hardly believable. Will the government dare to carry it out?
